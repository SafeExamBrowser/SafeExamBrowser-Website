About the Alliance

The SEB Alliance is the Contributor community that sustains with their contributions the ongoing funding of the Safe Exam Browser open source project to continue its maintenance, development and support activities. The Contributor fees are solely used for this purpose. ETH Zurich provides the infrastructure for the management and the software engineering of the SEB project and appoints an alliance manager. The SEB Alliance offers different contribution levels to parties interested in the evolution of the SEB open source project.  

More information about joining the Alliance is available in our benefits and documents section.
