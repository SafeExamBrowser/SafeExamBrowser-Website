Join the Consortium

 

  

If you are interested in joining the SEB Consortium please contact Nathalie Roth, the SEB consortium manager. You may hand in the membership application form to her, too:

SEB Consortium
c/o SWITCH
Nathalie Roth
PO Box,
CH-8021 Zurich,
Switzerland
+41 44 268 1567
consortium@safexambrowser.org

We will contact you back usually within three business days.

Due to legal constraints membership is currently open to European

  • educational and academic institutions (public and private)
  • foundations
  • public authorities
  • companies (for profit and non-profit)

with programs and missions consistent with the purposes of the Consortium.
Please note for questions regarding the use of the Safe Exam Browser Software or for downloading it please navigate to the website of the Safe Exam Browser open source project.
