Consortium Fees

Members Board Documents Benefits Fees Join Donate FAQ

Worldwide membership is open to European

  • educational and academic institutions (public and private)
  • foundations
  • public authorities
  • companies (for profit and non-profit)

with programs and missions consistent with the purposes of the Consortium.

Alternatively, organisations mentioned above may also make a donation if they do not wish to join the consortium, but would like to be recognised as a SEB supporter. Further information and the donation form can be found at the Donate to the SEB Consortium page.

Membership levels and annual fees of the SEB Consortium are as follows:

 

Educational and academic institutions, as well as foundations

Category

 

Annual Membership Fee

Who may join?

Benefits

Principal Members

EUR 50,000

 

Institutions of Higher Education, foundations

Prioritize SEB development goals, get direct, personal consultancy by the SEB developers. Logo of institution/company is placed on SEB consortium website, voting rights SEB Board

Gold Members

EUR 10,000

 

Institutions of Higher Education, foundations, other educational and academic Institutions

Suggest SEB development goals, build a feature list for SEB, get direct, personal consultancy by the SEB developers. Logo of institution is placed on consortium website

Silver Members

EUR 5,000

 

Institutions of higher Education, other educational and academic Institutions

Suggest SEB development goals, logo of institution is placed on consortium website

Bronze Members

EUR 2,500

 

Small academic Institutions

Logo of institution is placed on consortium website

All membership fees are due for payment on January 1st each year. ETH Zurich will bill the members and will make the debt collection. The financial year corresponds to the calendar year.
